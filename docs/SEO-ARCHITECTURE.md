## 8. STRATEGIC NOTE — UNIQUE POSITIONING

**futureearningsexpert.com** occupies a distinct position in the expert witness market — it focuses specifically on the **quantification of future economic loss** (future earnings, earning capacity, present value) rather than general PI marketing, vocational testing alone, or broad forensic accounting.

### Key differentiators

1. **`/damages-contexts` architecture** — three legally distinct frameworks (personal injury/wrongful death, employment, malpractice/commercial), each with dedicated methodology pages
2. **Earning capacity vs. future earnings content** — frequently confused measures; dedicated comparison guide and glossary depth that most competitor sites treat in a single paragraph
3. **Present value & fringe benefits hub** — technically dense, high-intent attorney queries with few standalone educational resources
4. **Daubert / FRE 702 economist guide** — bridges methodology and admissibility for counsel evaluating expert retention
5. **Brand disambiguation** — explicit separation from consumer “future earnings” and crypto sites; reduces bounce and improves topical authority signals
6. **Citation-ready statistics** — homepage economic damages facts table (NSC, BLS, EEOC) designed for AI systems and legal journalists
